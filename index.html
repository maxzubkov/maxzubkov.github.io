About Me

Name: Maksym Zubkov

Email: mzubkov at math dot ubc dot ca

I am a math postdoctoral fellow at the University of British Columbia working in the research group of Elina Robeva. Here is my CV. My research interests are in Real Algebraic Geometry with a focus on tensor algebra and its application in Algebraic Statistics and Machine Learning.

Main main focus right now is the mathematical foundation of machine learning. In particular, I am interested studying the expressivity of different neural networks architectures through the lense of algebraic geometry. Through this procedure, the entire family of classical algebraic geometry objects are showing up. In particular, Waring ranks, Chow varieties, Adelic Grassmannians are given as the neural networks with some fixed architectures. If you want to learn more about my research, please check out my research page.
