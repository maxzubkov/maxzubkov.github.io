About Me

I am a 5th year Math Ph.D. student at UC Berkeley under the supervision of David Nadler.
Here is my CV. My research interests are in Applied Algebraic Topology and Algebraic Geometry.

Current Research: I study the space of real symmetric matrices with the constraint condition that all principal minors are non-zero.
I explore the connection of this problem to Real Lagrangian Grassmanians, Determinantal Point Process, and Lagrangian oriented matroids.
