My Teaching Philosophy

Here is my teaching statement where you can read more about my approach as an educator. Mathematics is not about memorizing theorems and formulas. Those are just the notes and the shadow of the true spirit of the universe. One of the biggest challenges for a math educator is to build a bridge between the learning routine and a true understanding of the concept and to show how to overcome challenges along the way.
